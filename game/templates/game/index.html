The BuddyBikes Story

For 10 years, BuddyBikes has been a master of Industry 4.0. We used high-speed automation and data to build thousands of frames. We were fast, we were cheap, and we were profitable.

But the world has changed:
  • The Workers: Our team is exhausted. High-speed robots are efficient, but the human element—creativity and well-being—has been ignored.
  • The Planet: Our carbon footprint is too high, and modern customers are starting to walk away from dirty manufacturing.
  • The Market: One supply chain break shuts us down for weeks because we are too rigid.
Your Mission

Lead our Industry 5.0 Transition. This is a shift in mindset: Human-Centric, Sustainable, and Resilient.
